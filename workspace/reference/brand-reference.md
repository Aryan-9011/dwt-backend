## Design Style (from inspiration folders)

**Preferred aesthetic:** Scrapbook / travel journal / editorial collage

**Key design elements to use:**
- **Polaroid photo frames** — photos inside white polaroid-style borders, slightly rotated
- **Bold large destination text** — destination name as hero typography, often overlapping photos
- **Circular photo cutouts** — round-cropped destination photos arranged in grid or arc
- **Binder clip accents** — decorative binder clips holding "pinned" elements
- **Mixed fonts** — Bold sans-serif headlines + script/handwriting secondary font
- **Botanical/nature doodles** — small leaf, flower, star, sparkle decorative elements
- **Stamp overlays** — travel stamp graphics ("Time to Travel", destination stamps)
- **White notepad/paper cards** — key text on white card with slight shadow, pinned look
- **Collage grid** — multiple destination photos arranged in a grid with the brand identity

**Slide backgrounds:**
- Light blue `#e8f4f8` (matches DWT itinerary style)
- Or dark overlay on destination photo (60–70% opacity)

**DO NOT use:**
- Pure flat dark navy that looks generic
- Generic stock-photo corporate design
- Fonts that look like PowerPoint defaults
