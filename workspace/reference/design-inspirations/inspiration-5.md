# Design Inspiration 5 — Editorial Collage Carousel (Boketto Style)

**Style name:** Editorial Collage Carousel
**Best for:** Destination listicles, "top N places" awareness carousels, seasonal travel guides. Same use case as Inspiration 4 but with a rawer, more authentic travel journal feel.
**Reference output:** `reference/design-inspirations/inspiration-5-cover-sample.png`, `reference/design-inspirations/inspiration-5-inner-sample.png`
**Format:** Portrait 1080×1350px (Instagram standard)

---

## Design Philosophy

Raw editorial travel journal meets magazine poster. Inspired by @bokettotravels aesthetic.
- Cover uses a **white paper canvas with photo collage** — NOT a full-bleed photo
- Inner slides are **full-bleed photo with NO gradient** — text sits directly on the natural sky/light area of the photo
- Feels authentic and handcrafted, not polished or corporate
- Amber/gold circle is the signature graphic accent on the cover
- Typography is condensed, bold, dark charcoal — feels like a newspaper headline
- Two slide types only: Cover → Inner (repeatable). No separate CTA template needed (use a simple final inner slide as CTA)

---

## Two Slide Templates

---

### TEMPLATE 1 — Cover Slide

**Background:** Off-white textured paper with faint grid/graph paper lines. Warm paper tone. NOT a photo.

**Graphic element:** Large flat amber/gold circle (≈45% of image width) positioned center-right, behind the photo collage.

**Photo collage:** 4–5 cutout-style travel photos with natural edges, overlapping slightly, some slightly tilted. Positioned over and around the amber circle. Photos represent the destinations in the carousel.

**Layout — all left-aligned:**

```
[Small amber italic serif label]        e.g. "Summer 2026"

[MAIN HEADLINE LINE 1]                  [massive ultra-bold condensed dark charcoal]
[MAIN HEADLINE LINE 2]                  [same — e.g. "8 INTERNATIONAL" / "DESTINATIONS"]
[SUBHEAD]                               [bold navy condensed, smaller — e.g. "INDIANS ARE BOOKING"]
[DATE/SEASON LINE]                      [small navy — e.g. "MAY & JUNE 2026"]

[PHOTO COLLAGE + AMBER CIRCLE]         [lower half of image]

[Scattered 5–8 small black bird silhouettes across the image]

@dreamworldtours                        [bottom center, small dark serif]
```

---

### TEMPLATE 2 — Inner Slide (Repeatable for each destination)

**Background:** Full-bleed cinematic real photo of the destination. Vivid, atmospheric. The photo MUST have a lighter/sky area at the top where dark text will be legible.

**Overlay:** NONE — no gradient, no dark overlay. Photo fully visible.

**Layout:**

```
[LEFT BORDER]  [DESTINATION NAME]      [top-left — thin 3px dark charcoal vertical line + large bold condensed dark charcoal headline]
               [Descriptor line]       [small regular italic white or light text below headline]



                                        [XX / YY]    [top-right, small clean white light weight]




@dreamworldtours                        [bottom center, very small]
```

**Critical rules for inner slides:**
- Destination name must land on the LIGHT/SKY portion of the photo — dark charcoal text needs contrast
- Thin vertical left border line (3px, ~60px tall, dark charcoal) runs left of the destination name
- Descriptor line is italic, regular weight — NOT bold
- Only ONE descriptor line (unlike Inspiration 4 which has two)
- Slide counter top-right always
- NO boxes, NO panels, NO gradient overlay
- Photo selection matters — MUST have clear sky or light area at top for text legibility

## Typography Rules

- **Cover headline:** Ultra-bold, condensed, dark charcoal — fills the top portion of the slide
- **Cover subhead:** Bold condensed navy — visually distinct from the main headline
- **Cover italic label:** Small amber italic serif — adds editorial flair
- **Inner destination name:** Large bold condensed dark charcoal — NOT white (contrast against sky)
- **Inner descriptor:** Small italic white — gentle, poetic, one line only
- **Nothing is teal** — this style uses charcoal, amber, and navy only. No teal.

---

## Photo Direction Per Inner Slide

The photo MUST have a bright/light sky or light background at the top for dark text legibility:
- Daytime shots work best (bright sky available)
- Avoid night shots, heavy forest shots, or dark moody images for inner slides
- Aerial shots with sky visible at top are ideal
- Golden hour works if sky is still light enough

---

## What NOT to Do

- NO gradient overlay on inner slides — the whole point is the photo breathes fully
- NO teal color anywhere — wrong style
- NO white text headline on inner slides — dark charcoal on light sky only
- NO bottom text placement on inner slides — text always top-left
- NO multiple descriptor lines — one only
- NO solid colored cover background — must be off-white paper texture
- NO amber circle on inner slides — only on cover
- DO NOT use this style for offer/product posts — purely for awareness/listicle carousels
